Argumentation-based MAL

Enrique Plaza is one of the few researchers who unites the areas of multi-agent learning, case-based reasoning and argumentation. Machine argumentation, also known as defeasible reasoning [must click link to understand] and machine learning are rather disjoint fileds, because argumentation is symbolic while learning typically is numeric. (New topic for 2013.)
