Essentials of game theory

Game theory (as: “Von-Neumann-Morgenstern economic game theory”) is an important prerequisite of multi-agent learning. This year [ed.: 2017-18], the multi-agent learning course is offered in Block 2 rather than in Block 4. Because of that, the multi-agent learning course is scheduled prior to the multi-agent systems course (Block 3). The latter discusses a great deal of game theory. Therefore, the first week of MAL is used to get on a par with the essentials of game theory. You are free to skip the first lectures if you are well-versed in game theory. On the other hand, I am pretty sure that even if you are comfortable with game theory, it is always beneficial to brush up your knowledge and to tune in with the notation. You may even learn something new.
