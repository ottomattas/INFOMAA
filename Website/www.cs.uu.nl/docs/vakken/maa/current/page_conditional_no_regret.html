Conditional no-regret

A player has regret if it could have earned more by playing a certain action throughout. A player has conditional regret if it could have earned more by substituting a certain action in its past play by another action (throughout). An important general result in this area is that it is possible to define algorithms that eliminate conditional regret irrespective of the behaviour of opponents.
