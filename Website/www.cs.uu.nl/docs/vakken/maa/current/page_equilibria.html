Types of equilibria

Some multi-agent learning algorithms, such as regret matching (RM, Hart & Mas-Colell), converge, not to Nash equilibria, but to more general types of equilibria, such as so-called correlated equilibria and coarse correlated equilibria. It is therefore worth the effort to study these new types of equilibria.
